The Sweet Spot's FAQs

Is there a limit to how many Monthly Deals I can pre-order?

Yes, the quantity varies each month dependant on what the deal is. You will always find the limit under the price calculator.

Do you have Gluten-Free products?

Yes, we have a small variety of Gluten-Free products ranging from cookies, cupcakes, cakes, and loaves of bread. All of our Gluten-Free products are marked with a blocked out wheat symbol.

Do you give your recipes out?

No, every recipe is a secret recipe passed down from generation to generation. However on some of our newer products we were not the ones to create it. Just ask the cashier and they can help you with a select recipe guide for your favorites.
